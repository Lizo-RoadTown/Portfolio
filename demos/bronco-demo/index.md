# Welcome to the FRAMES Demo for Bronco Space Lab

**For:** Lab Director & Program Leadership
**Purpose:** Decision briefing on FRAMES deployment at Bronco Space Lab
**Time to Complete:** ~15 minutes

---

## What This Demo Covers

This walkthrough will show you how FRAMES can solve critical challenges facing Bronco Space Lab—student rotation, knowledge loss, overwhelmed team leads, and unpredictable mission outcomes.

**You'll learn:**

1. **The Problem** — What structural challenges are causing failures
2. **The Solution** — How FRAMES predicts and prevents these failures
3. **The Architecture** — How the system works technically
4. **Implementation** — What it takes to deploy at BSL
5. **Next Steps** — How to approve and proceed

---

## Why This Matters Now

Bronco Space Lab faces NASA mission deadlines with rotating student teams. Every semester:

- Critical knowledge graduates without documentation
- New members take months to become productive
- Team leads spend more time training than engineering
- Interface failures emerge during integration (too late to fix)

**FRAMES makes these problems predictable and preventable.**

## About FRAMES

**FRAMES** (Framework for Research & Analytics in Mission Engineering Systems) is a multi-university research platform that predicts mission success by mapping organizational structure.

- **Lead Institution:** Cal Poly Pomona
- **Partner Universities:** 8 institutions across the US
- **Focus:** University space programs with NASA contracts
- **Status:** Research phase with functional prototype
